Moments from SANHSP's History

SAHSP’s Establishment: A Glimpse from the Past

SANHSP, a 4-storey building for secondary education located in Barangay San Antonio, Parañaque, was created to address the growing need for schools that could provide quality education to a growing population of learners. The school officially opened on June 1, 2015, with 22 sections for Grade 7. It also operated as an annex of Paranaque National High School-Main.

Vision for the Future

The establishment of SANHSP aims to provide secondary education to graduates from local elementary schools and less fortunate students from Barangay San Antonio and neighboring areas. It also addresses the overcrowding in Paranaque National High School-Main, with a vision to contribute to the wider goal of education for all and improve access to quality education in the region.

Achievements and Recognition

Since its establishment, SANHSP has continuously made strides in improving the quality of education, winning numerous awards and recognitions in various academic, sports, and cultural competitions. Over the span of five years, the school has gained recognition from the City of Parañaque and neighboring cities in Metro Manila.

Independence and Growth

On November 16, 2016, the Regional Evaluation Team visited the school and encouraged the school to design innovations for greater independence. Following these insights, SANHSP gained independence from Parañaque National High School-Main on January 1, 2017. Today, the school continues to thrive, with a focus on academic excellence and holistic development.
